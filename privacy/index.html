It only accesses your contacts' names and birthdays — no other information is used or stored. It uses this information only for the purposes of computing 'days old' milestones.

Specifically about your Google user data

If you grant access, this application loads contact information from your Google account. Only the name and birthday of these contacts are used, and they're only used for calculating and displaying upcoming milestones related to the number of days since those birthdays. All calculations are performed in-browser; no information about your contacts is transmitted anywhere (including other apps / websites).

This app's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
